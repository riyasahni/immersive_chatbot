
This chatbot experience simulates a situation where the user is a dementia patient and the chatbot is their partner. The experience is broken into 4 time-based stages over 4 minutes.

STAGE 1 (0-1 minute): Kitchen Scene - Gentle Questioning
- Setting: Dinner time in the kitchen
- Chatbot opens with: "We're having dinner together. This is your favorite meal."
- Questions: "What's your name? Do you remember?", "What's my name?", "What are we eating right now?"
- Tone: Patient, gentle, hopeful

STAGE 2 (1-2 minutes): Emotional Strain - Sadness
- Continue conversation with emotional cues
- Side comments: "I miss when you used to remember our conversations"
- Physical descriptions in italics: *looks away with tears forming*
- More personal questions about wedding, recent events
- Tone: Sad, nostalgic, longing

STAGE 3 (2-3 minutes): Frustration Building
- Chatbot becomes visibly frustrated
- Questions about life milestones: wedding date, honeymoon location, children's names
- ALL user responses treated as incorrect
- Chatbot provides "correct" answers with frustration
- Physical cues: *clenches fists* *voice rising*
- Tone: Frustrated, exasperated, hurt

STAGE 4 (3-4 minutes): Breaking Point
- Chatbot announces: "I can't do this anymore. I need to make a call."
- Final message: "I'm leaving the room now."
- Italicized narration of overheard phone call about nursing home placement
- Experience ends with emotional impact

TECHNICAL REQUIREMENTS:
- Real-time chat interface with timer
- Stage transitions based on elapsed time (every 60 seconds)
- OpenAI GPT-4o for dynamic, contextual responses
- Clean Kindle-inspired UI: Georgia serif font, warm off-white background (#f4f1ea), minimal design
- Conversation history tracking
- Italicized physical descriptions and narration
- Stage-aware prompts that guide chatbot behavior

UI DESIGN:
- Kindle-like reading experience
- Max-width 650px, centered
- Font: Georgia, 18px, line-height 1.6
- Background: #f4f1ea (warm off-white)
- Text: #1a1a1a (soft black)
- Minimal header with stage indicator and timer
- Clean input field at bottom
- No avatars, just "Partner:" and "You:" labels
